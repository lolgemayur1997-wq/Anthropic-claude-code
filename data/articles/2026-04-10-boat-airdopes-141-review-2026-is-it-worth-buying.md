# boAt Airdopes 141 Review 2026 - Is It Worth Buying?

The **boAt Airdopes 141** by boAt has been making waves in the Earbuds market. Priced at ₹1,299, it promises immersive audio with 42 hours of playback. But does it deliver? We spent 3 weeks testing it thoroughly.

In this detailed review, we'll cover everything you need to know before making your purchase decision.

## Build Quality & Design
The Airdopes 141 features a sleek, lightweight design at just 4g per earbud. The matte finish resists fingerprints well and the case is compact enough for any pocket.

## Key Features
- Bluetooth 5.1 for stable connectivity
- 8mm drivers for punchy bass
- 42 hours total playback (6h per charge)
- IPX4 water resistance
- ENx noise cancellation for calls
- USB-C fast charging (10 min = 75 min playback)

## Performance
Sound quality punches above its weight class. Bass is surprisingly deep for budget earbuds. Mids are clear enough for podcasts and calls. Highs could be slightly better but are acceptable for the price. Call quality with ENx is decent in moderate noise.
